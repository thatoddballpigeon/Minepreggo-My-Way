## Brewing Tips

- **Redstone Extension:** Adding Redstone to any potion extends its duration
- **Multi-stage Recipes:** Some potions require multiple brewing steps to reach maximum strength (Tier V)
- **Ingredient Variety:** Different life substances create different effects on creatures
- **Progressive Amplification:** Each tier uses a different ingredient:
  - Tier 2: Glowstone Dust
  - Tier 3: Nether Quartz Ore
  - Tier 4: Blaze Rod
  - Tier 5: Ender Pearl

# Impregnation Potions - (AI-generated content)

## Overview

Impregnation potions are special drinkable items in the Minepreggo mod that instantly trigger pregnancy in the player or mob that drinks them. Unlike waiting for natural pregnancy triggers, these potions provide immediate and reliable pregnancy initiation. Each potion has different effects depending on its strength level and the type of species it's designed for.

## How Impregnation Potions Work

### Basic Mechanics

1. **Drinking the Potion**
   - Player or mob consumes an impregnation potion
   - The potion applies an invisible effect to the drinker for approximately 15 seconds (300 ticks)
   - This duration allows the potion effect to naturally expire

2. **Natural Expiration (Key Trigger)**
   - When the potion effect expires naturally after 15 seconds, it triggers pregnancy
   - **Important:** If the effect is removed before expiring (milk effect, removed manually, etc.), pregnancy will NOT occur
   - Only natural expiration counts!

3. **Impregnation Occurs**
   - Upon natural expiration, the entity is impregnated based on the potion's strength level
   - The drinker immediately enters the Pre-Pregnancy phase
   - Number of babies depends on the potion's amplifier level

## Who Can Use Impregnation Potions?

### Players
- **Female players:** Can drink potions to become pregnant with ANY species type
  - Impregnation Potion → Human babies
  - Zombie Potion → Zombie babies
  - Creeper Potion → Creeper babies
  - Any other species potion works
- **Male players:** Cannot become pregnant (potions have no effect)
- Must not already be pregnant or in post-pregnancy phase
- Must have female reproductive capabilities

### Mobs (Preggo Mobs) - Species Compatibility Restrictions
**Important:** Preggo mobs have strict species compatibility. They can only be impregnated with babies of their own species OR with human babies (through natural sex with a male player).

- **Zombie Girls:**
  - ✅ **Compatible with:** Zombie babies (Impregnation Potion or Zombie Impregnation)
  - ✅ **Compatible with:** Human babies (only through sex with male player)
  - ❌ **Incompatible with:** Creeper, Ender, Villager potions
  - Can drink potions if not in post-pregnancy

- **Creeper Girls:**
  - ✅ **Compatible with:** Creeper babies (Impregnation Potion or Creeper Impregnation)
  - ✅ **Compatible with:** Human babies (only through sex with male player)
  - ❌ **Incompatible with:** Zombie, Ender, Villager potions
  - Can drink potions if not in post-pregnancy

- **Ender Girls:**
  - ✅ **Compatible with:** Ender babies (Impregnation Potion or Ender Impregnation)
  - ✅ **Compatible with:** Human babies (only through sex with male player)
  - ❌ **Incompatible with:** Zombie, Creeper, Villager potions
  - Can drink potions if not in post-pregnancy

### Creatures That Cannot Use Potions
- Villagers (they have different breeding mechanics)
- Baby mobs (immature forms cannot become pregnant)
- Already pregnant entities
- Entities in post-pregnancy/recovery phase

## What Happens When You Drink a Potion

### Timeline

**0 seconds:** Player/mob drinks the potion
- Invisible effect applied
- Drinker feels no immediate change
- Effect starts counting down (15 seconds = 300 ticks)

**1-14 seconds:** Potion effect active
- Visual particles may appear around the entity
- If milk is consumed or effect is removed during this time, pregnancy will NOT occur
- Drinker can move around normally

**~15 seconds:** Potion effect expires naturally
- Pregnancy is immediately triggered
- Pre-pregnancy phase begins
- For general **Impregnation Potion:** Babies match the mother's species
- For species-specific potions: Babies are of the potion's specified species
- Number of babies depends on the potion's amplifier level

## Important Details

### Impregnation vs. Natural Pregnancy

| Feature | Impregnation Potion | Natural Pregnancy |
|---------|-------------------|------------------|

| **Reliability** | Very reliable | Depends on luck |

- Drinker is already pregnant

- Drinker is not a valid female entity
- Drinker is a baby creature

### Drinker Must Be:
- Female reproductive capacity
- Not currently pregnant
- Not in post-pregnancy phase
- Adult (not a baby)
- Capable of pregnancy

## Technical Notes

### Effect Duration
- All impregnation potions have a 15-second (300 tick) duration
- Effect is hidden: `false` in visibility
- Effect is ambient: `true` (no particle effect shown to player)
- Amplifier is the determining factor for baby count

### Marking System
- The mod uses an internal tracking system to mark entities for impregnation
- Only entities marked for impregnation are processed when the effect expires
- If an effect is removed by other means, the mark is automatically cleared
- This prevents accidental impregnation from removed effects

### Effect Removal
- If milk is consumed during the 15-second window, the pregnancy mark is removed
- Manually removing effects will also cancel impregnation
- This gives players/admins control over whether pregnancy occurs

### Species-Specific vs. General Potion
- **General Impregnation Potion:** Uses the mother's species to determine baby type
  - Perfect for maintaining breed purity (zombie with zombie, creeper with creeper, etc.)
  - Players always get human babies
- **Species-Specific Potions:** Override the mother's species and force a specific species
  - Used to create hybrid babies (e.g., zombie mother + creeper potion = creeper babies)
  - Allows cross-species breeding

## Practical Usage Tips

1. **Confirm Pregnancy:** Wait for the effect to wear off (about 15 seconds) - you'll see visual changes and messages confirming pregnancy

2. **For Pure Breed:** Use the general **Impregnation Potion** - babies will match the mother's species automatically

3. **For Mobs - Species Restriction:** Preggo mobs can ONLY use potions that match their species or the general Impregnation Potion. Using an incompatible potion will damage them instead.

4. **For Players - All Potions Compatible:** Players can use ANY potion type to get the corresponding species babies

5. **Hybrid Creation:** Hybrid babies (mixed species) can ONLY occur through natural sex events between different species, not through potions with mobs
